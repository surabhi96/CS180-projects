Project 1: Focal Length and Perspective (iPhone 12)

This project explores how zoom levels (0.5× to 1×) on the iPhone 12 affect perspective. Below are my results for Part 1 (selfies), Part 2 (architecture), and Part 3 (dolly zoom).

Part 1: Selfie – The Wrong Way vs. The Right Way

I captured selfies at different zoom levels. The wide angle (0.5×) produces distortion, while stepping back and using higher zooms (closer to 1×) produces more natural portraits.
